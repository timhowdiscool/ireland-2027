## hero.jpg — the splash photo (optional but recommended)

The site ships with a hand-built emerald→stone gradient hero that looks good on its own.
To make it sing, drop a real photo here named exactly **`hero.jpg`** and it will fade in
over the gradient automatically — **no code changes needed**.

Good choices (landscape, high-res, ~2000px+ wide):
- Cliffs of Moher
- The Wild Atlantic Way / Ring of Kerry
- Connemara or the Dingle Peninsula
- Anything green, dramatic, and Irish

Where to find freely-usable photos: [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/ireland),
[Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/search/ireland/) (both free for commercial use, no attribution required).

The image is positioned `center 42%` and darkened slightly at the bottom for text legibility.
If your photo needs a different crop, tweak `.hero-photo { background-position }` in `style.css`.
